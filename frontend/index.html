How Recovery Works

A secure, automated system designed to eliminate unstructured WhatsApp groups and prioritize privacy.

01

Submit a Report

Detail the item you lost or found. Owners must provide a hidden verification detail known only to them.

02

System Matching

Our algorithm cross-references attributes like category, color, location, and keywords to identify high-probability matches.

03

Admin Verification

The admin reviews algorithmic clusters and cross-references the hidden verification detail against the physical found item.

04

Secure Notification

Once verified, the system automatically emails both parties securely. The owner can collect the item from the designated office.
